Yale University
May 12 – 14, 2023

venue

SALT 33 will be held on site at Yale University, in the Yale Science Building and Luce Hall. Both are located on Hillhouse Avenue, described by Mark Twain and(/or?) Charles Dickens as "the most beautiful street in America". The poster session and conference dinner will be held at Hotel Marcel, known for its iconic Bauhaus design and commitment to sustainability.

travel

Comprehensive information about getting to New Haven is available here

  • New Haven is served by the NYC area airports (JFK, Laguardia, Newark) in addition to Hartford's Bradley International (BDL).
    Most convenient however is the Tweed-New Haven Airport (HVN, an Avelo Airlines base)

parking

Parking is free on Sundays in all of New Haven, including Yale campus. Free on-street parking is available everyday on Canner Street and north of it [see map]. Metered parking is available closer to the venue but there are time limits that make it somewhat inconvenient.

food

The closest and cheapest options are food trucks at the intersection of Sachem and Prospect (5 minutes) as well as on Grove and Hillhouse (10-12 minutes)
Within a 10-15 minute walk are restaurants on Whitney, between Trumbull and Grove. Restaurants on State Street are similarly a 15 minute walk.
There are many restaurants around Chapel Street, Crown Street, Broadway etc, but they are a good 25 minutes walk. Definitely worth exploring for dinner (as they would be close to your hotels, but perhaps less ideal for lunch.

Something interesting: New Haven Night Market
