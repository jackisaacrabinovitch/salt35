This is the 33rd iteration of Semantics and Linguistic Theory. For more general information on the goals and history of the conference, visit the permanent website at http://saltconf.github.io

Assuming the travel policies of Yale University, the state of Connecticut, and the United States all permit it, we expect presentations at SALT33 to take place in person.
